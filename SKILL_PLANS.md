# Runecraft — Skill Expansion Plans

Concrete build plans for the under-developed skills flagged in the alpha audit:
current gap → proposed level ladder → every new node/recipe/item/object with its
ID, level, XP, **where it goes in worldgen**, and the **art it needs**. Art rows
are mirrored into `ART_RUNESCAPE.md`; textures into `TEXTURE_PACK.md`.

Design targets: ladders reach ~L90+, no dead zones > ~15 levels, every product
has a sink, every station is reachable in the *endless* world (not just the
tutorial). XP curve reference: ~2.7k XP to L10, ~59k to L30, ~258k to L50.


## Gathering — Foraging, Hunting, Archaeology

### skill.foraging

**Problem now:** Foraging has exactly ONE node: resource.bush.berry (L1, 8xp, hands-only Thicket Berries). It caps instantly at L1 and is the worst skill in the game — nothing to gather past the first minute, no tool progression, no biome variety. It already scatters widely (endless.ts cases 1,3-oasis,7,10,15,21,23,24,26,31 and plains default) so the plumbing is proven; it just has no ladder. Fix: a 10-tier RuneScape-berry bush ladder L1->L90 across biomes, berries edible so Foraging feeds itself, plus a Secateurs tool gate at L30 for tool progression.
