# Characters & Factions — the Stoneleaf Reach

## Factions

| Faction | Role | Leader | Symbol / look | Wants | Disagrees with |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Roadwrights' Guild** | player's guild; menders of spans and roads | Guildmother **Essa Harrow** (Bellbrook) | crossed hammer & rope on slate grey | fix what people need; understand the Braid before re-powering it | Relight's haste; Magpie looting |
| **The Relight** | restoration movement | Provost **Serah Vane** | gold flame on deep red | every Braid station relit at full power | anyone slowing "the return of the good years" |
| **Magpie Company** | salvage crews | Captain **Corvo Bray** | black-and-white pennant | coils and components, sold to the highest bidder | contracts that don't pay |

| **Delvers' Compact** | miners & smiths | Compact-Master **Brann Coalward** (Highcairn) | pick over anvil | safe deep works; the Liftworks running | Relight requisitions |
| **Chronicle House** | archivists | Keeper **Odessa Quill** | open book & lantern | the Sundering understood and published | both sides hoarding records |

Faction standing is expressed through dialogue, quest availability and shop
access — no numeric reputation system (per project scope).

## Dialogue style

- Short spoken lines (toast-length), plain speech, no lore dumps.
- Every named NPC has at least 2 idle lines that carry world texture.
- Vane never reads as cartoonish; Bray is funny until he isn't.
