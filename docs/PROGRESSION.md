# Progression & Resource Matrix — the Stoneleaf Reach

## Skills (existing 9, target bands 1–60 across the acts)

| Skill | Prologue–Act I | Act II | Act III+ | Story integration |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Woodcutting | oak (1), spruce (2), birch (3) | acacia (4) | jungle (6+) | bridge beams for spans/causeways |
| Mining | copper (1), tin (2) | iron (4), mountain lodes | badlands lodes (⏳ gold/deep) | clearing tunnels, stone for repairs |
| Fishing | shore & lake | river & delta spots | warm coast, Palewick | feeds settlements (Mirefen chain) |
| Foraging | berries | swamp gleaning | jungle & mushroom fields | antidote makings (⏳ Brewing) |
| Cooking | campfire basics | inn hearths | expedition rations | quest food |
| Smelting | copper/bronze | iron | ⏳ higher tiers | anchor fittings |
| Smithing | bronze tools | iron tools/armor | ⏳ specialist | span hardware, coil seats |
| Attack / Defense | cave beasts | swamp/wolves | constructs, bosses | the acts' fights |
| ⏳ Farming, Brewing, Enchanting, Archaeology, Construction, Archery | — | — | designed in acts II–IV | see WORLD_BIBLE acts |

Unlock cadence: every 2–5 levels a new node tier, recipe, region resource or
quest uses the skill (existing recipe gates: Smelting 4, Smithing 5–8, etc.).
The main story requires only moderate levels (Woodcutting ~5, Mining ~4,
Attack ~8 by Act II); everything higher is optional advantage.

## Tool tiers

improvised → **copper** → **bronze** → **iron** (all shipped) → ⏳ gold/
specialist → ⏳ deep-earth tier (Act IV+). Earlier materials stay useful:
planks and stone bricks are the repair currency of span quests; copper
remains the arrow/fitting metal (⏳).

## Economy

Shops: Bellbrook general store (existing) + **Tanglewood Post** (timber &
hides), **Mirefen Fishmonger** (catch & rations), **Sunward Exchange**
(ores, gems, monster materials). Monster drops are sellable trade goods;
critical quest items are not sellable (no shop buys them).
